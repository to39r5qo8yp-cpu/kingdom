## Qualification Criteria (Customizable)

**Hot Lead** — Book call immediately
- Budget confirmed
- Timeline under 30 days
- Decision maker contact
- Requested demo/pricing

**Warm Lead** — Follow up within 24 hours
- Expressed interest
- Timeline 30-90 days
- Right company size

**Cold Lead** — Nurture campaign
- Just browsing
- No timeline
- Not a fit currently
